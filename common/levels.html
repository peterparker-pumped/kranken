How does the staking work?

We offer four staking pools with different conditions. To participate in IDOs you need to stake TPAD in one of them to obtain a level.

There are a few important characteristics to consider:

APR - how profitable are the rewards you receive for staking
Lock duration/maturity - for how long you can't unstake your TPAD without a penalty
Early withdrawal fee - how much TPAD we take for unstaking from a locked stake

You can combine multiple pools, level is calculated based on a sum of your TPAD locked in pools. Once you stake enough, you are free to register for as many IDOs as you want.

Be aware: a deposit gets immediately locked. The good news is: once locked, the unlock date won't change with other IDO registrations. Only when your deposit is unlocked, it can be locked again by another registration in an IDO.
